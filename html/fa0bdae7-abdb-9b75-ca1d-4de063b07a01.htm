The DiscordVoiceConfig type exposes the following members.

Properties
  NameDescription
Bitrate Obsolete.
The voice client now auto-configures this based on the channel.
Channels
The amount of channels you wish to send over the network. Use 2 for stereo. Stereo will require more bandwidth.
FrameLengthMs
The frame length, in ms, of audio you'll be sending at one time. As a rule of thumb, you generally want to set it to something above your current Discord ping.
OpusMode
The mode the Opus encoder will use. MusicOrMixed is generally the best.
PCMBlockSize
The blocksize of PCM data you should be reading and piping into SharpCord.
SendOnly
If true, the voice client will only send voice and will not receive it.
